Colour Me


Isn't life just utterly overwhelming? It's like a vibrant and intricate painting, full of colors and details that sometimes blur into a mess. We're constantly juggling between our dreams, responsibilities, trying to find that elusive balance.


I've decided to make a public commitment to myself, a promise to stay accountable and monitor my journey through this labyrinth. I have this tendency to develop a laser focus on one aspect of my life, pouring all my energy and passion into it. While that dedication has led to some achievements, it often came at a cost.


I've seen how my intense pursuit of one goal could inadvertently neglect other crucial areas of my life. It's like a see-saw; when one side soars, the others dip, and the overall balance gets disrupted.


So, I've embarked on a journey to discover the intricate threads that connect health, career, and relationships. I'm here to share my insights, my challenges, and my triumphs.


In my pursuit of balance and growth, I've established three key gauges: Health, Career, and Relationships, each with three critical subcategories. Regular scoring on a scale from 1 to 10.

Health Hues

Health has become my top priority, especially as I approach my 29th birthday in 2024. It's time to get serious about my well-being! I'll admit, I've fallen into some not-so-great habits—ordering takeout, not moving enough, and forgetting to stay hydrated.


Body

I've prioritised regular exercise and a reasonably healthy diet because:


1. Boosted Energy Levels 💥: Regular exercise pumps up your endorphins, giving you more energy to enjoy daily activities. Imagine feeling like a Duracell bunny 🐰, ready to hop around and enjoy life to the fullest!

2. Adventure-Ready Body 🚵‍♀️: Whether it's hiking up a mountain 🏔️, going on a spontaneous road trip 🚗, or trying out a new sport 🏄‍♂️, a fit body is always ready for an adventure. It's like being a superhero 🦸‍♂️ in your own action movie.

3. Sharper Mind 🧠: Regular physical activity and a healthy diet can sharpen your mind, improve concentration, and enhance creativity. You'll be solving puzzles and coming up with brilliant ideas like a modern-day Einstein 🕵️‍♂️.

4. Stress Buster 💆‍♀️: Exercise is a fantastic stress reliever.

5. Social Butterfly 🦋: Group sports, dance classes, or just jogging with a friend are great ways to socialize.

6. Confidence Booster 👑: Achieving fitness goals and feeling good about your body can skyrocket your confidence.

7. Better Zzzs 🌙: Regular exercise can help you sleep better.

8. Age Gracefully 🌟: Staying fit can help you look and feel younger.

9. Fun Wardrobe 👗: As you get fitter, you'll have more options for fun, fashionable clothing.

10. Tasty and Healthy Foods 🥗: Exploring healthy recipes can be a culinary adventure.

11. Dance Like Nobody's Watching 💃: Improved stamina and flexibility mean you can dance longer and wilder.

12. Playful Life 🎉: With more energy and better health, even routine tasks can become playful and enjoyable.

Workout Plan

Monday, Wednesday, Friday (Strength and Core Focus):

Morning or Evening (30-45 minutes)

Warm-Up: 5-10 minutes light cardio (jogging, jumping jacks)
Circuit (Repeat 3x):
15 squats
12 lunges (each leg)
10 push-ups
15-second plank
12 bicycle crunches
Cool Down: Stretching

Tuesday, Thursday (Cardio and Flexibility):

Morning or Evening (30 minutes)

Warm-Up: 5 minutes brisk walk
Cardio: 20 minutes (jogging, cycling, or brisk walking)
Cool Down: 10 minutes of yoga or stretching

Saturday (Active Recovery):
Light activity like walking, swimming, or a leisurely bike ride.

Sunday (Rest Day): No structured exercise. Focus on rest and recovery.
